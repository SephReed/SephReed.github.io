The Hexapod

by: Leland, Seph, Dom, and Misty
The Hexapod is an infinitly mirrored galaxy housed in a 10' aluminum octahedron. The outside is built as a glistening monolith beaconing participants to explore more. Upon entering the structure guests will find a fractured universe of stars, lights, and mirrors. By manipulating a spire that juts through the center of the structure, guests can augment the cosmos to fit their dreams.

Our goal is to build different versions of the Hexapod, using the same exterior form and unique interiors to create a large multifaceted installation that grows and changes over time. Hexapods can stack in an infinite crystaline pattern, so each one we make will connect to the ones made before.
