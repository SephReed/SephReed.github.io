Ceaser Cipher

Take every letter of a message and shift it some constant amount around the alphabet. As in a -> b, b -> c, etc. The number of shifts is the key. You can decode a Ceaser Cipher by checking for letter frequency or by brute force, trying every shift between 0 and 26.
