Who I am

  Here's a few of my qualities: empathy, experience, drive, and like a smidge of humor.
  Empathy being more than a simple reflection of those around you, it is the ability to hear a story and fill all the characters shoes, searching for the misunderstandings. It is the cornerstone of human centered design and ethics.
  Skill is something better shown than said. If you would like to judge mine for yourself, see my portfolio, and take note that every project was made from 100% scratch. The code contains no external libraries, the sound designs don't use presets, the physical builds aren't from tutorials, and the image assets are never stock.
  As for humor, it is the Ying to stress's Yang. It's very serious to me that people not be too serious. Seriously. Things are going to be okay, and let's all have a laugh.
  And finally, real talk, of all metrics of personal success I can express, to me the most involved and complex is that I manage to be content and laugh and joke and care while simultaneously being aware of just how much corruption exists, and how truly and utterly quelled most of our dreams are. To me, that is a success that even the most intelligent rarely accomplish.
