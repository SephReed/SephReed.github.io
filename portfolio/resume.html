Hey, I'm Seph and I'm trying to understand and be capable of doing everything. Because of this, my interests and skills are all over the place. I've done a lot of odd jobs and it usually comes as a surprise when I'm the guy who can park the trailer or fix pretty much anything. I also learn incredibly quickly because I've started from scratch so many times.

Of all the things in life I value, I think the most important is that I'm not wasting my time. Life is short, but the experiences are vast, diverse, and endless.

Ideally, I would like a job that is up to date technologically and strives to create a symbiotic relationship between itself and its customers.

Idealistically, I would like to work with music and those kinds of things which impact people in deep, long lasting ways.

Overall, I'd just like to leave this world improved and have a good time while I'm at it.
